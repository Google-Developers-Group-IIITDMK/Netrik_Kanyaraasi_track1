## Instructions to Save as PDF

1. Open `ARCHITECTURE_DIAGRAM.html` in your web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox)
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac) to open Print dialog
3. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
4. Adjust settings:
   - Layout: Portrait
   - Margins: Default
   - Scale: 100%
   - Background graphics: Enabled (important!)
5. Click "Save" and name it: `Architecture_Diagram_Kanyaraasi.pdf`
